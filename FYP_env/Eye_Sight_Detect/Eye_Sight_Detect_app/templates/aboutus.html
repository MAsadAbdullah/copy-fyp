About Us

NesMasPoint Is a Classified Ads Marketplace which provides sellers and business owners an avenue to advertise their products and services to potential buyers with little fees or no fees option. The Marketplace offers a user-friendly interface that allows sellers to upload images and details of their goods and services, including the location, size, price, colour, status, etc. Interested buyers can search for products based on their preferences and contact the seller directly through the platform. NesMasPoint provides a convenient and efficient way for sellers and buyers to connect, saving time and resources. NesMasPoint Classified Ads Marketplace covers a wide range of categories, including real estate, vehicles, jobs, electronics, household items, pets, services, beauty and make up, and more, catering to different budgets and preferences. Whether you are a first-time property buyer or an experienced investor, on NesMasPoint Classified Ads Marketplace you can find the perfect property, goods, and services that meet your needs and aspirations.
